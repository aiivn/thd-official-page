01

Students

Our desire for the younger generation is a self-awarenes, independent and mature. That’s why we launched Social & Emotional Learning for this age group.

In addition, we also launched the Science Film Festival with the aim of stimulating the interest and understanding of science in children. From there, they will learn more about the world around them through what they learn.

02

Teachers

We offer training programs focusing on two major topics: Social & Emotional Learning (SEL) & STEM. And two major projects that THD focus on deployment are Science Fim Festival and SEAD:STEM.

We have the opportunity to train with teachers across the country on topics such as how to develop an SEL application; Developing a cross-disciplinary project with the spirit of STEM …. We hope to bring more useful knowledge to the school, so that teachers and students can experience quality content from the world.

03

Parents

THD’s biggest blessing is working with many students across the country. Since then, we have been in contact with many parents of children 1-12 grades and at the university.

The family is a large school that directly influences the development of students; Because of this importance, we focus on sharing with parents about SEL, how to listen to children, how to be friends with children, how to feel safe when talking with parents. We hope to see a lot of changes, from the very least, starting with the family.

Meet the team behind
THD Edu Solutions

DUONG HOANG

Founder & CEO, SEL & STEM Trainer

Hoang Duong has a bachelor’s degree from the Hanoi University of Pedagogy in 2006. He has held the chairmanship of the Student Union of the Hanoi University of Pedagogy, he has 15 years of experience in design and organization as well as directing various types of programs such as music, exchanges, games, experiences and studying through movies. He is also a core lecturer of many teacher training programs of the Ministry of Education and Training.

He chooses to research and develop training programs based on SEL and STEM. He has been joining with SFF since 2014. He is also an Social & Emotional Learning Training Specialist with more than 4,000 hours of experience for over 50,000 students, students, parents and teachers across the country.

KHUYEN TO NGUYEN

Educator, Lecturer & THD Expert

Khuyen Thi To Nguyen received excellent certification for undergraduate degree in Hanoi University of Education in 2013, Master course degree in 2016 in Faculty of Physics, Hanoi University of Education. She has a passion for science, especially for Physics.

She always finds the best method to take science is very dear to pupil’s heart. She was a teacher in WE LOVE SCIENCE Club in Alpha secondary school. Now, she is not only learning for Master Course but also teaching science in Academy for creation which has many science courses for pupils.

QUYNH TRANG NGUYEN

Educator, Lecturer & THD Expert

Trang Quynh Nguyen held a master of Film and Television studies. She is a lecturer at the Hanoi Academy of Theatre and Cinema with experience in teaching, professional scriptwriting, film and television production.

She has produced many fun learning programs for children on Vietnamese Channels. She also works for the Centre of Research, Support and Development of Culture in the capacity of setting up and managing film development projects in Vietnam. She has joined Science Film Festival since 2011 and worked with thousands of children all over the country. She wishes to further her career in education and art for children.

MINH DUC DO

STEM Teacher, THD Expert

Minh Duc is a talented, enthusiastic and passionate Science teacher. Started as a Life Skills Training Specialist, he has more than 3 years of experience in developing Social & Emotional learning programs for students of all ages and he also gives special interest for students from 1 -6 grades. Having passion for the equipments and machinery; he always wondered what role technology plays in modern life and how he can use them in teaching.

For the first time, he started to learn about STEM with all his heart. With a passion for science, along with a sense of self-study as well as high self-control; he’s been widening his knowledge and using them right in his classes. He tried and tried, and tried again. So far, he has had more than 5 years of experience in developing STEM programs in schools and classes.

Having expericence as well as the passion for STEM, Minh Duc’s choice to accompany THD is a contribution to a vision that THD is always aiming for. He believes that STEM is an effective way to train his students to be more passionate, more ready for the changes in their future lives, and to embrace the era of technological advancement.

DUY THANH DOAN

Technologies Expert

Duy Thanh graduated with a degree in Computer Science. He has a passion for Education, Science & Technology.

He is currently a Product Specialist at Google Asia Pte. Ltd. He has over 3 years of experience in implementing and consulting Enterprise Technology Systems based on the application of technology from Google as well as contributing many open source projects. He chose Google’s projects and technology as the basis for his extensive research.

He has joined the Science Film Festival project (a project that THD is a partner) since 2016 with many roles. He also has more than 5 years of experience of working in the education environment, from which he understands the difficulties, challenges and joys of Training. He chooses to be a part of THD’s vision as a Technology Advisor.

He believes the harmony between Social and Emotional Learning and STEM will bring about the image of a kind citizen; with refinement and insight, with the ability to be nimble, adaptable and ready for changes from the world in the future; especially when the beginning of the technological era is coming.
